About Us

Sprachcafé Nürnberg was founded by Olivier Ishimwe, in October 2022.

"I got my inspiration from an exchange language group in Regensburg. It's called Sprachcafé as well and I really enjoyed the concept. It is different from other exchange groups out there, where people meet and just talk and drink. In Regensburg, we had papers and pens, writting down all new words doing some games related to languages while having fun!"
- Olivier I.

The event has grown up, averaging 35 to 40 people per event. We are now a team of 3 people in charge of organizing the event.

Alot of Projects are in the process of development :

  • Introducing themed events, where we will focus on one language, learn about about people who speaks it and a bit of history.
  • Reading events : People will read texts from their languages(even languages which are not on the program). The focus will not be to understand, but to listen and appreciate the sounds.
  • Music events, in the group we have talented people who are looking forward to play music from their different origins.
