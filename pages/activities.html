Activities

Here are some activities that can be helpful on your table, of course you are allowed to use your creativity, suggest other interesting activities, only the sky's the limit!!!

Level 1
  • Alphabet,Pronunciations, numbers(1-5), Hellos, thank yous, …
  • Look around the room and find five objects(activities) that you don’t know. translate it. write down the translation.
    Exchange that paper with your table neighbor and try to challenge each other until one of you completes all the words without looking at the paper.

Goal : Unforgettable first steps in a new language… don’t give up!

Level 2
  • Level 1 exercises.
  • Self Introducing : your name, where you are from, where you live, your occupation, your hobbies, your dream job, dream destination,...
  • Schedule a meeting with a friend(days, month, year, hours).

Goal : To have what it takes to impress "a little bit" native speakers!

Level 3
  • Level 1 and Level 2 exercises.
  • Describe an image or a location to somebody.
  • Speaking about Holidays, hobbies, sharing your experience in Germany( for foregneirs), ...
  • How to guide someone to go from point A to point B.

Goal : To have what it takes to travel and live for a short time in the natives speakers countries.

Level 4
  • Level 1 and Level 2 exercises.
  • Describe an image or a location to somebody.
  • Speaking about Holidays, hobbies, sharing your experience in Germany( for foregneirs), ...
  • How to guide someone to go from point A to point B.

Goal : To have what it takes to travel and live for a short time in the natives speakers countries.

Level 4

  • Talk about stereotypes. And sharing opinions on them.
  • Talk and debate about social, political subjects.

Goal : To be able to live and work in the native speakers countries.
